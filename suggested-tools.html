Suggested Tools

Essential Tools

These tools will allow you to complete most projects in this collection:

  • Circular saw or track saw
  • Cordless drill/driver
  • Measuring tape and square
  • Clamps (at least 4)
  • Sandpaper or orbital sander
  • Pencil and marking tools

Recommended Upgrades

These tools will improve accuracy and efficiency:

  • Table saw for repeatable cuts
  • Router for edge profiles and joinery
  • Random orbital sander for finishing
  • Brad nailer for assembly
  • Pocket hole jig
  • Digital angle finder

Materials & Supplies

  • Plywood (typically 3/4" and 5/8" for most projects)
  • Wood glue
  • Wood screws in various lengths
  • Wood filler
  • Finishing materials (stain, polyurethane, or paint)
  • Safety equipment (glasses, dust mask, hearing protection)

3D Printing (Optional)

Some projects include 3D printed hardware components. If you have access to a 3D printer:

  • FDM printer (Prusa, Ender, etc.)
  • PLA or PETG filament
  • Basic slicing software knowledge

Pre-printed components may also be available for purchase.
