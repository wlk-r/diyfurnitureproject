About

The Project

diy furniture project is a material and virtual design initiative that seeks to make building durable, functional furniture more accessible to everyone. By combining traditional woodworking techniques with modern 3D modeling and fabrication methods, we provide detailed plans and guidance for creating quality furniture pieces.

Philosophy

We believe that well-designed furniture should be accessible, affordable, and built to last. Our designs prioritize:

  • Simple construction methods using common tools
  • Readily available materials like plywood and standard hardware
  • Modular designs that can be adapted to different spaces and needs
  • Structural integrity and longevity over trends

Approach

Each furniture design begins as a 3D model, allowing for precise measurements and visualization before any material is cut. The digital models can be viewed and manipulated in your own space using AR technology. Detailed plans include cut lists, assembly instructions, and finish recommendations.

We incorporate 3D printed components where they improve functionality or simplify construction, but the core structure relies on time-tested joinery and building techniques.
